Detailed Description

Class corresponding to the IafTauCell schema type.

Integrate and fire cell which returns to its leak reversal potential of leakReversal with a time constant tau
:param leakReversal: :type leakReversal: voltage :param tau: :type tau: time :param thresh: The membrane potential at which to emit a spiking event and reset voltage :type thresh: voltage :param reset: The value the membrane potential is reset to on spiking :type reset: voltage
