Detailed Description

Class corresponding to the IafCell schema type.

Integrate and fire cell with capacitance C, leakConductance and leakReversal
:param leakConductance: :type leakConductance: conductance :param leakReversal: :type leakReversal: voltage :param thresh: :type thresh: voltage :param reset: :type reset: voltage :param C: Total capacitance of the cell membrane :type C: capacitance
