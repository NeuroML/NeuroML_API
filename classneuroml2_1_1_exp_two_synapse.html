Detailed Description

Class corresponding to the ExpTwoSynapse schema type.

Ohmic synapse model whose conductance waveform on receiving an event has a rise time of tauRise and a decay time of tauDecay. Max conductance reached during this time ( assuming zero conductance before ) is gbase * weight.
:param tauRise: :type tauRise: time :param tauDecay: :type tauDecay: time :param gbase: Baseline conductance, generally the maximum conductance following a single spike :type gbase: conductance :param erev: Reversal potential of the synapse :type erev: voltage
